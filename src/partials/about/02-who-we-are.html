Who We Are

Serious business finance has always been a luxury of scale. The FP&A talent that turns numbers into strategy usually sits inside large multinationals, the Unilevers and P&Gs of the world because only they can afford to keep it in-house. Smaller businesses go without it, not for lack of need, but because that talent is priced out of reach.

Frontier Quotient changes that. We democratize high-end FP&A and business finance giving growing companies the same financial muscle the biggest players run on, sized and priced for where you are today.
