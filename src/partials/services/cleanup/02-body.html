WHAT WE DO

From backlog to clean slate

It happens more often than anyone admits. Months — sometimes years — of transactions pile up unreconciled. Reports stop matching reality. The books become something everyone avoids looking at too closely, and every decision gets made on gut feeling instead of actual data. The longer it goes, the harder it feels to fix.

We dig in and sort it out. Our cleanup and catch-up service takes your accounting from wherever it is now to where it needs to be: current, accurate, and reliable. That means working through the backlog systematically — reconciling accounts, correcting misclassifications, rebuilding broken reporting structures, and closing out the periods that have been left hanging.

The goal isn’t just to get you caught up once. It’s to hand you back a set of books you can actually trust, with a clean foundation for staying current going forward. Once the cleanup is done, you’re in a position to make real decisions again — and to keep your records that way.

What you get

  • Full historical bookkeeping cleanup and reconciliation
  • Transaction reclassification and corrections
  • Rebuilding of chart of accounts and reporting structures
  • Prior-period financial statement preparation
  • System migration cleanup (QuickBooks, Xero, etc.)
  • Transition plan to ongoing bookkeeping maintenance
